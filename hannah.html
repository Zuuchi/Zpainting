Hannah Vania

My name is Hannah Vania, and I am excited to showcase my artworks that revolve around the two fundamental themes of identity and climate change. In exploring the subject of identity, I have delved deep into the various aspects of culture that play a crucial role in shaping our identity. My artworks reflect the diverse, rich, and complex nature of culture, and how it influences our sense of individuality. I have explored the different cultural practices, traditions, beliefs, and values that shape our identity. Through my art, I aim to highlight the importance of embracing and celebrating our cultural heritage, while also acknowledging the need for change and growth. When it comes to climate change, my focus has primarily been on its causes and the impact it has on the environment. I have created artworks that shed light on the devastating consequences of neglecting the environment and the importance of taking measures to protect it. My artworks also showcase the beauty of nature and how it can flourish when we take the necessary steps to preserve it. Through my art, I aim to inspire others to take action and make a positive impact on the environment.
