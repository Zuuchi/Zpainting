Mary Tonda

Hello! My name is Mary Tonda, and I am a Year 12 student studying BTEC art. Art has always been a passion of mine, starting from when I was young. But it was during the COVID-19 pandemic lockdown in Uganda in 2020 that I really decided to take my passion seriously. During that period, I spent most of my time teaching myself how to draw realism. It was a challenging but rewarding experience that has shaped my artistic skillset and perspective. Since then, I have been practicing my art by creating my own works and re-creating the works of famous artists. I believe that practicing is crucial for growth in any field, and art is no exception. By practicing, I have been able to hone my skills and develop my own unique style. To me, creating art is more than just a hobby. It's a passion that allows me to express myself in a way that I cannot through other forms of communication. I love bringing my ideas to life and creating pieces that resonate with myself and others. Whether I'm working on a drawing or a painting, I always strive to create something that is beautiful, meaningful, and inspiring.
